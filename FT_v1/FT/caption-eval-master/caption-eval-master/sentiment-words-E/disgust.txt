恶心：
1. disgust： a strong feeling of dislike or disapproval for sb/sth that you feel is unacceptable, or for sth that looks, smells, etc. unpleasant


恶心的：
1. disgusting: arousing revulsion or strong indignation.
2. disgusted: very annoyed or upset by something that is not acceptable.
3. nasty: highly unpleasant, especially to the senses; physically nauseating.

恶心地：
1. disgustedly： with disgust
2. disgustingly： in a disgusting manner or to a disgusting degree
