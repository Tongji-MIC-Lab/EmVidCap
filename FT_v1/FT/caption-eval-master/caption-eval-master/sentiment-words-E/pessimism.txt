悲观：
1. pessimism： a feeling that bad things will happen and that sth will not be successful; the tendency to have this feeling

悲观的：
1. pessimistic：tending to see the worst aspect of things or believe that the worst will happen.
2. gloomy： dark or poorly lit, especially so as to appear depressing or frightening.
3. depressed： (of a person) in a state of general unhappiness or despondency.

悲观地：
1. pessimistically： Someone who is pessimistic thinks that bad things are going to happen.
